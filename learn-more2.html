Learn More About Creative Thinking

Creative thinking is the ability to look at problems or situations from a fresh perspective and come up with innovative solutions.

What is Creative Thinking?

It's a way of looking at problems or situations from a new angle. It’s about generating new ideas, exploring possibilities, and thinking outside the box.

Why is it Important?

Creative thinking helps students solve complex problems, adapt to new situations, and innovate in their learning and future careers.

How to Develop It?

Practice brainstorming, ask “what if” questions, explore different viewpoints, and don’t be afraid to make mistakes or experiment.
